The Student Grievance Support System (SGSS) portal is used for the Students to
report their Grievance and issues if they affected by some difficulties.
Student can report their Complaints under University/College/Department levels
based on their Complaints the Action will be taken by the Committee Members.
A committee member will read the Students Complaints and they validate the
complaints.based on the validation committee member will mark it read, spam, reply throug
h email, and the Complaints will be taken to the Head if the issues go on serious. The Head will take action under the Government of AP
